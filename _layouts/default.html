Guangying Ma

gma7 at uic dot edu

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at University of Illinois Chicago. I joined the Biomedical Optics and Ophthalmic Imaging Laboratory in 2019 under the supervision of Dr. Xincheng Yao. 

I am currently studying the functional imaging of the retina. My research includes optical coherence tomography (OCT) imaging system reconstruction, image processing and biomedical data analysis.

I am currently investigating the different technqiues involved in optical system reconstruction and medical image signal analysis in the mouse and human retina. 

For more details, please see my exciting list of publications!
